Disney films are full of good sidekicks.

Our Favorites

Timon

Timon, the wise-cracking meerkat from The Lion King, brings humor and charm while teaching us Hakuna Matata — no worries!

Genie

Genie from Aladdin, voiced by the legendary Robin Williams, is a whirlwind of jokes, impressions, and magical shenanigans.

Dory

Dory’s short-term memory issues make her endearing and hilarious. She’s the loyal friend who always tries her best — just keep swimming!
